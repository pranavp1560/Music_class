About the Academy

Where musicians are made, not just taught

Generation Music Academy trains students across Guitar, Piano, Keyboard, Drums, Violin and Vocal performance, with one belief at the center: technique only matters once it can hold a stage. Every program moves through the same arc — learn, practice, perform, become — so progress is something students can feel, not just hear about in a report card.

With branches in Kolhapur, Sangli and Ichalkaranji, lessons are built around individual attention from mentors who perform professionally themselves, not just teach from a syllabus.
