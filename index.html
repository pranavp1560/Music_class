OUR STORY

Where Passion Meets Professional Excellence

Founded with a vision to transform music education in Maharashtra, **Generation Music Academy** is not just a coaching class—it is a modern sanctuary for artists. We believe music is meant to be felt, lived, and performed.

From custom-tailored individual attention to state-of-the-art instrument practice spaces, our branches in **Kolhapur, Sangli, and Ichalkaranji** cultivate beginners into professional musicians ready to capture any stage.

YOUR ROADMAP

The Student Journey

How we guide you step-by-step from an aspiring music enthusiast to a confident performer.

1

Join Academy

Begin with a free trial diagnostic class to evaluate your musical aptitudes and objectives.

2

Learn Basics

Master critical music notation, posture, scales, and core finger structures on your instrument.

3

Practice Routines

Utilize soundproofed academy rooms for mentored practice, speed, and accuracy refinement.

4

Performance Training

Participate in group jam sessions, rehearsal workshops, and mic technique training.

5

Stage Shows

Perform live before real audiences at local concert halls, cultural festivals, and academy recitals.

6

Become Musician

Graduate with recognized certifications, a performance portfolio, and direct recording studio access.
